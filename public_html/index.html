About Us

Thomas

Thomas graduated with a Bachelors in Computer Science from Georgia Tech in 2017.

He interned with Union Pacific Railroad for 3 years during his undergrad and now works there full time. Thomas is part of the team of engineers responsible for designing the core network infrastructure that enables communications within UP's datacenters and out to rail yards and trains across the western two-thirds of the United States.

In his free time Thomas enjoys computer programming, gaming, and the occasional photography and cycling.

Demi

Demi also graduated from Georgia Tech in 2017, with a Bachelors in Industrial Engineering.

She now works for Michelin full time as an Industrial Engineer, optimizing manufacturing operations in a tire retread facility.

Our Story

Thomas and Demi met early in their college careers, but never really talked much one-on-one until their Junior year at a birthday party for one of their best friends, Dana. At that point things seemed to click between them and they started spending lots of time together watching a TV show they both enjoyed. Eventually Thomas got the courage to ask her to spend time with him off campus, and Demi joined him at a shooting range for an outing with the Georgia Tech Marksmanship Club. A week later they had their first date.

Travel

Ashland, NE is about a 30 minute drive from Omaha, we recommend flying into OMA and booking a hotel near I-80 in Omaha. More details to come regarding hotel blocks.

From Atlanta:

  • Delta provides nonstop flights multiple times a day between ATL and OMA starting around $350 round trip

  • Southwest also has flights for a little cheaper, starting around $300 round trip with a layover in St Louis or Chicago

From Seattle:

  • Alaska Airlines provides nonstop flights between SEA and OMA that range between $240-350 depending on date

  • United and American both offer multiple flights under $300 with layovers

From Greenville:

  • Delta provides flights between GSP and OMA with a layover in ATL starting as low as $225 round trip

From Detroit:

  • United has flights between DTW and OMA with a layover in ORD starting around $265 round trip
